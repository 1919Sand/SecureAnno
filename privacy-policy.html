How SecureAnno Handles Inquiry Data

This page explains what information we collect from this website and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit the contact form, we may collect your name, work email, phone number, company name, job title, company size, service interest, estimated data volume, project details, IP address, and submission timestamp.

2. Why We Collect It

We use this information to respond to your inquiry, prepare quotes, understand project requirements, maintain lead records, and improve how we support prospective clients.

3. Notification & Storage

Inquiry details may be stored on SecureAnno infrastructure and forwarded to internal email inboxes or configured business notification channels used for lead handling and customer support.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your inquiry data only with service providers involved in hosting, email delivery, or business workflow automation, and only as needed to operate the website and respond to you.

5. Retention

We retain inquiry data for as long as reasonably necessary to evaluate business opportunities, provide follow-up, comply with legal obligations, and maintain operational records.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email hello@secureanno.com.
